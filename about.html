#WhoIsJerryDixon

I'm a former insurance professional turned UX/UI Designer motivated by helping others effectively achieve their goals through innovative, functional and inspirational designs across multiple platforms.

I’m originally from Fresno, CA, where I attended CSU, Fresno and I am currently located in Phoenix, AZ - by way of Atlanta, GA. I am inspired by travel, taking risks and conquering the fear of the unknown.

I’m not only impassioned by getting from point ‘A’ to point ‘B’, but I also enjoy every detail in between while continuously improving the process by which to do so.
